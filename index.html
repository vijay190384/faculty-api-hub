🎯 What is API Score & Why Does It Matter?
ℹ️
API = Academic Performance Indicator — a numerical score calculated from your research outputs, used by UGC and AICTE to determine eligibility for CAS (Career Advancement Scheme) promotions from Assistant Professor to Associate Professor to Professor.
✅
Who needs this? Every faculty member applying for promotion under UGC CAS. Your API score is checked during every promotion — insufficient score = promotion rejected, regardless of years of service.
  • Promotion from Asst. Prof. Stage 1 → Stage 2 → Stage 3 requires minimum API scores
  • Score is checked for each assessment period (usually 3–5 years)
  • Both total score AND specific mandatory requirements must be met
  • Missing even ONE mandatory item can block promotion

📰 Journals — Valid Parameters
⚠️
UGC CARE List ABOLISHED — October 3, 2024. The UGC 584th meeting officially discontinued the CARE journal whitelist. Now, any Transparent Peer-Reviewed journal with valid ISSN, impact factor, and ethical publishing is valid. Your institution (HEI) evaluates quality per UGC suggestive parameters. Scopus/WoS indexing remains the gold standard.
Journal TypeScoreValid DocumentsKey Rule
🏅 Scopus / WoS Q135 ptsDOI + Scimago Q1 screenshot + acceptance letter1st / corresponding author = full pts. Co-author = 10 pts
🥈 Scopus / WoS Q225 ptsDOI + Scimago Q2 confirmationVerify quartile at scimago.org before submission
📊 Scopus / WoS Q3/Q410 ptsDOI + Scopus author page screenshotLower quartile but still Scopus indexed — valid
📄 Peer-Reviewed / Refereed Journal5 ptsISSN, editorial board visible, impact factorPost-Oct 2024 UGC rule. Institution validates. Avoid predatory journals (check Beall's list)
💡 Patents — All 3 Stages Explained
ℹ️
Patents are scored at 3 stages — Filed, Published, Granted. You move up as the patent progresses. Filing alone satisfies the mandatory requirement and earns 10 pts immediately.
StageScoreWhat to SubmitTimeline
📮 Patent Filed10 ptsIPO application number + filing receiptImmediate after filing. Points upgraded later.
📰 Patent Published15 ptsOfficial Gazette reference number~18 months after filing. Replaces filed score.
🏛️ Patent Granted – India25 ptsIPO Grant Certificate with grant number2–5 years. Co-inventors share equally.
🌍 Patent Granted – International30 ptsUSPTO / EPO / PCT Grant CertificateHighest IP score. Institution name earns bonus credit.
🎨 Design Registration10 ptsCGPDTM Design Registration CertificateSeparate from patent. Counts independently.
